# <span id="Celsius:_a_community_resource_for_Affymetrix_microarray_data" class="mw-headline">Celsius: a community resource for Affymetrix microarray data</span>

Paper by Allen Day, Marc RJ Carlson, Jun Dong, Brian D O'Connor and
Stanley F Nelson. Appeared in Genome Biology 2007, 8:R112
doi:10.1186/gb-2007-8-6-r112

## <span id="Abstract" class="mw-headline">Abstract</span>

Celsius is a data warehousing system to aggregate Affymetrix CEL files
and associated metadata. It provides mechanisms for importing, storing,
querying, and exporting large volumes of primary and pre-processed
[microarray](Category:Microarrays "Category:Microarrays") data. Celsius
contains ten billion assay measurements and affiliated metadata. It is
the largest publicly available source of Affymetrix microarray data, and
through sheer volume it allows a sophisticated, broad view of
transcription that has not previously been possible.
