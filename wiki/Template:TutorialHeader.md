# <span dir="auto">Template:TutorialHeader</span>

<div id="bodyContent">

<div id="siteSub">

</div>

<div id="contentSub">

</div>

<div id="jump-to-nav" class="mw-jump">

</div>

<div id="mw-content-text" class="mw-content-ltr" lang="en" dir="ltr">

Template to create a header for a tutorial page.

Specify the variables thus:

    {{ TutorialHeader
    | what = <tool name>
    | where = <conference/venue name; wikitext OK>
    | when = <date>
    | who = <presenter; wikitext OK>
    | logo = <link to logo>
    }}

and they will come out looking like this:

<div class="indent">

\<Centered logo\>

This \<what\> tutorial was presented by \<who\> at the \<where\>,
\<when\>. The most recent \<what\> tutorial can be found at the \<what\>
Tutorial page.

</div>
