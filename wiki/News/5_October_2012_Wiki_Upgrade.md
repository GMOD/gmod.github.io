# <span dir="auto">News/5 October 2012 Wiki Upgrade</span>









The GMOD wiki will be going offline on October 5th at 5 PM PST to update
the software to the most recent release of Mediawiki. Any wiki edits
performed during the upgrade will be lost, so please finish your wiki
page edits before the upgrade starts.

Update: the upgrade is complete, bar a few minor modifications. Please
contact the GMOD helpdesk at help@gmod.org if you discover any problems.
Thanks!
