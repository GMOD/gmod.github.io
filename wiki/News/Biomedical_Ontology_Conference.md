# <span dir="auto">News/Biomedical Ontology Conference</span>





From GMOD









Jump to: [navigation](#mw-navigation), [search](#p-search)





The <a href="http://icbo.buffalo.edu/" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">International Conference on Biomedical Ontologies
(ICBO)</a>, will be held in Buffalo, New York from July 23-26, 2009.
Ontologies are widely used in GMOD for annotation, controlled
vocabularies, reasoning, and data sharing.
<a href="http://icbo.buffalo.edu/Call_for_Papers.pdf"
class="external text" rel="nofollow">Paper submissions</a> are due March
1, and
<a href="http://icbo.buffalo.edu/Poster.pdf" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">posters</a> are due April 10.
<a href="http://icbo.buffalo.edu/" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">ICBO</a> will be held in conjunction with <a
href="http://www.bioontology.org/wiki/index.php/Tutorials_and_Classes"
class="external text" rel="nofollow">four days of tutorials and
classes</a> from July 20-23, 2009.

See the <a href="http://icbo.buffalo.edu/" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">ICBO conference web site</a> for details.
