<a href="../PAG_2011#Roadshow" rel="nofollow"

GMOD will have a large number of representatives at [Plant and Animal
Genome (PAG)](../PAG_2011 "PAG 2011") in San Diego this month. Three of
them,

- [Dan Blankenberg](../User:DanB "User:DanB") of the
  [Galaxy](../Galaxy.1 "Galaxy") project,
- [Mitch Skinner](../User:MitchSkinner "User:MitchSkinner"), lead
  [JBrowse](../JBrowse.1 "JBrowse") developer, and
- [Scott Cain](../User:Scott "User:Scott"), GMOD Project Coordinator

are available to visit organizations in the San Diego area during the
conference.

If you would like to arrange a visit with **Dan, Mitch**, and/or
**Scott** to discuss **[Galaxy](../Galaxy.1 "Galaxy"),
[JBrowse](../JBrowse.1 "JBrowse")**, or any other **[GMOD
Components](../GMOD_Components "GMOD Components")**, please contact
<a href="mailto:dan@bx.psu.edu" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">Dan</a>,
<a href="mailto:mitch_skinner@berkeley.edu" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">Mitch</a> and
<a href="mailto:scott@scottcain.net" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">Scott</a> directly.

[Dave Clements](../User:Clements "User:Clements")  
[Galaxy Project](../Galaxy.1 "Galaxy")
