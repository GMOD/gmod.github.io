[<img src="../../mediawiki/images/2/26/Calendar_icon.gif" width="36"
height="35" alt="GMOD Calendar" />](../Calendar "GMOD Calendar")

</div>

The [next 5 weeks](../Calendar "Calendar") includes the a
[MAKER](../MAKER.1 "MAKER") workshop at the
<a href="http://www.k-state.edu/agc/symp2010/" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">Arthropod Genomics Symposium</a>, and deadlines for
<a href="http://www.iscb.org/ismb2010-deadlines" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">ISMB</a> (Boston in July),
<a href="http://www.amatameeting.org/" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">AMATA</a> (Hobart in September),
<a href="http://gi-workshop.net/" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">GIW</a> (Hangzhou in December),
<a href="http://www.eccb10.org/" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">ECCB</a> (Ghent in September),
<a href="http://incob10.hgc.jp/" class="external text"
rel="nofollow">InCoB</a> (Tokyo in September), and
<a href="http://meetings.cshl.edu/meetings/infouk10.shtml"
class="external text" rel="nofollow">Genome Informatics</a> (Hinxton in
September).

  
If you know of any other events or deadlines that the GMOD community
would be interested in, please send them to me.

Thanks,

[Dave Clements](../User:Clements "User:Clements")  
[GMOD Help Desk](../GMOD_Help_Desk "GMOD Help Desk")
