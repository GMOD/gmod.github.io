# <span id="The_CMap_Comparative_Map_Browser_and_Displaying_Population_distributions_with_GBrowse_and_PhyloGeoViz" class="mw-headline">The CMap Comparative Map Browser and Displaying Population distributions with GBrowse and PhyloGeoViz</span>

Presentation by [Ben Faga](User:Faga "User:Faga") at the <a
href="http://ccg.biology.uiowa.edu/smbe/symposia.php?action=view&amp;sym_ID=27"
class="external text" rel="nofollow">SMBE 2009 GMOD Workshop</a> on
displaying frequency data by geolocation in
[GBrowse](GBrowse.1 "GBrowse"), and a general overview of
[CMap](CMap.1 "CMap").

This presentation is also available in
[OpenOffice](File:CMapGBrowseSMBE2009.odp "File:CMapGBrowseSMBE2009.odp")
and [PDF](File:CMapGBrowseSMBE2009.pdf "File:CMapGBrowseSMBE2009.pdf")
formats.
