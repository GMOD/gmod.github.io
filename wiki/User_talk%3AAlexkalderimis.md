# <span id="Mashing-up_InterMine" class="mw-headline">Mashing-up InterMine</span>

Alex' talk covered the following topics:

- A general introduction to InterMine
- The general principle of decoupling we aim to implement
- Problems with implementing cross-domain webservices, and the JSONP
  workaround
- A walk-through of our new JavaScript client library
- An introduction to our new central registration webapp
