Face Caucus ApiDB: User Studies and Impact on Development

ApiDB: User Studies and Impact on Development, by

  • Eileen Kraemer, University of Georgia
  • Steve Fischer, University of Pennsylvania

In this talk we describe a set of user studies performed at a user workshop, the results of those studies, and the impact on the development of the ApiDB web sites. A “tour” of new features will be included. We conclude with a summary of useful approaches to user studies in the context of bioinformatics databases.
