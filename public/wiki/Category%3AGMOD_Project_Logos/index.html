Category%253AGMOD Project Logos

The official GMOD logo looks like this:

It is available in a number of different sizes and formats; we advise you use the highest quality version where possible. Please contact the GMOD help desk for custom configurations and sizes.

The original GMOD Logo was created by Simon Twigger of RGD. Most of the derivative logos created between 2007 and 2010 were done by Dave Clements during his time at the GMOD Help Desk, and those created between 2012 and 2014 were by Amelia Ireland.

You are encouraged to use these graphics in your presentations, posters, and web sites. See GMOD Promotion and Logos and Icons for more.
