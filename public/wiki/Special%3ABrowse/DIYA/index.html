Has description DIYA (Do-It-Yourself Annotator) is a module … DIYA (Do-It-Yourself Annotator) is a modular and configurable open source pipeline framework, written in Perl, used for the rapid annotation of microbial genome sequences. The software is currently used to take nucleotide sequence contigs as input, either in the form of complete genomes or the result of shotgun sequencing, and produce an annotated sequence. === Status === Active development of DIYA is currently on hiatus, pending the hiring of additional people to work on it.
