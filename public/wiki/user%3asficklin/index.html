Stephen Ficklin

Bioinformatics Research Associate for the Main Bioinformatics Laboratory at Washington State University. (WSU) Formerly at the Clemson University Genomics Institute (CUGI). Stephen leads the team that develops the Tripal web front end for Chado.

Tripal

Tripal is a collection of Drupal modules under development at WSU, the University of Saskatchewan and CUGI that serve as a web front-end for Chado databases. Tripal initially started as a web front-end for the Marine Genomics Project (MG.org).
