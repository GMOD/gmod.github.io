PAG 2012

GMOD had a strong presence at the Plant and Animal Genome Conference (PAG XX). There was again an all-day dedicated GMOD Workshop at PAG. The workshop was in addition to the hands-on GBrowse tutorial that was also offered. As in past years (see 2011, 2010, 2009), there were also many workshops, computer demos, and posters, either about GMOD and GMOD Components, or using GMOD components.
