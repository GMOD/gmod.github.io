PAG 2009

GMOD once again had a strong presence at the Plant and Animal Genome Conference (PAG 2009). PAG was also immediately before the January 2009 GMOD Meeting and many GMOD members attended both meetings.

There were be many, many workshops, computer demos, and posters, either about or using GMOD Components. This page lists only those items that mentioned GMOD Components in their abstracts.
