News/Galaxy Events: April 2011

There are a number of Galaxy-related events going on this month:

  • Intro to Galaxy workshops, April 6 at University of Pittsburgh, Dan Blankenberg
  • Dynamically Scalable, Accessible Analysis for High-Throughput Sequence Data, April 13 talk at BioIT World, Boston, Enis Afgan
  • NBIC Galaxy Hackathon, April 13-14, Belgium; this is now full but they are still accepting proposals to work on.
  • Early registration closes for 2011 Galaxy Community Conference, April 24. Register early and save 20%.

Dave Clements
Galaxy Project
