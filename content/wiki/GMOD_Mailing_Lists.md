# GMOD Mailing Lists

This page lists most of the mailing lists relevant to GMOD. The default location for GMOD related lists is [SourceForge](http://sourceforge.net/mail/?group_id=27707), but lists are actually implemented on various servers all over the world.

## Searching Email Lists

GMOD's default mailing list server is Sourceforge. Sourceforge, however, has a notoriously hard to use mail archive search facility. Therefore, we try to archive mailing lists on sites that have friendlier search facilities, such as [Nabble](http://gmod.827538.n3.nabble.com/), [Gmane.org](http://gmane.org), and [Google groups](http://groups.google.com). The tables below link to these archives, whenever they exist.

## Posting to and Membership in Email Lists

Most GMOD lists require you to be a member of the list to post to the list. If you aren't a member, your post will be *deferred* until one of that list's managers has a chance to review your posting. Therefore, *we strongly suggest that you join a list before posting to it.*
