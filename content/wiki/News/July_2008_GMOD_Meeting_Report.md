# News/July 2008 GMOD Meeting Report

[<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/GMOD/gmod.github.io/main/mediawiki/images/3/31/GMOD2008Discussion.JPG"
width="245" height="148" alt="July 2008 GMOD Meeting" />](../July_2008_GMOD_Meeting)

The [July 2008 GMOD
Meeting](../July_2008_GMOD_Meeting) was held on
July 16-17, 2008 at the University of Toronto. The meeting was attended
by over 30 people representing more than 20 different organizations. The
meeting report and links to presentations are now available on the [July
2008 GMOD Meeting](../July_2008_GMOD_Meeting)
page. Several [GMOD Components](../GMOD_Components)
were covered, including
[Chado](../July_2008_GMOD_Meeting#Chado), the
[Community Annotation
System](../July_2008_GMOD_Meeting#Community_Annotation_System),
and [Table
Editor](../July_2008_GMOD_Meeting#Table_Editor)
(plus several more). We also heard reports from many [GMOD
users](../July_2008_GMOD_Meeting#GMOD_User_Community),
including [SGN](../July_2008_GMOD_Meeting#SGN),
[WikiMods](../July_2008_GMOD_Meeting#WikiMods.org),
and
[Xenbase](../July_2008_GMOD_Meeting#Xenbase)
(plus several more).
