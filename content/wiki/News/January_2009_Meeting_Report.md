# News/January 2009 Meeting Report

[<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/GMOD/gmod.github.io/main/mediawiki/images/b/b6/Jan2009Meeting.JPG" width="319"
height="100" alt="January 2009 GMOD Meeting" />](../January_2009_GMOD_Meeting)

The [January 2009 GMOD
Meeting](../January_2009_GMOD_Meeting) was
held January 15-16, 2009 in San Diego, immediately following the [Plant
and Animal Genome (PAG)](../PAG_2009) conference. The meeting
was attended by over 50 people representing about 35 different
organizations. The meeting report and links to presentations are now
available on the [January 2009 GMOD
Meeting](../January_2009_GMOD_Meeting) page.
There were [19
presentations](../January_2009_GMOD_Meeting#Presentations)
by GMOD users and developers.

Some highlights:

- How best to [share
  data](../January_2009_GMOD_Meeting#Data_Sharing)?
- [BioMart](../January_2009_GMOD_Meeting#BioMart)
  integration with other GMOD Components.
- [JBrowse](../January_2009_GMOD_Meeting#JBrowse)
  update
- [WebGBrowse](../January_2009_GMOD_Meeting#WebGBrowse:_GBrowse_Configuration_Management) -
  [GBrowse](../GBrowse.1) configuration on the web.
- A [Drupal interface to
  Chado](../January_2009_GMOD_Meeting#Drupal_and_MarineGenomics.org).

Plus 14 other excellent user and developer presentations.

Please take a look at the [meeting
report](../January_2009_GMOD_Meeting) for an
update on what's going on in the GMOD community.

We hope to see you at the upcoming [August 2009 GMOD
Meeting](../August_2009_GMOD_Meeting), in
Oxford UK (and our first meeting in Europe).
